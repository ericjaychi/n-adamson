ABOUT ME

Welcome! Being a gamer at an early age, I was fascinated with the excitement and adventure of being transported to another world. Video games and films, presented to me, an accessible route into realms of fantasy and awe, where anything was possible. It was this feeling of wonderment and limitless boundaries that I became immersed in not only fantastic story-telling, but the intricate visualization of worlds.

As a 2-D environment/concept artist based in Ontario, California, I help visualize worlds and settings that strive to present something new and memorable for viewers. Since graduating from UC Irvine and attending Gnomon School of Visual Effects, I have been working as a freelance artist since 2014. I enjoy figuring out how things work and combining different elements of ideas to create something unique and new. I like inventing places that clients have never seen before because I want to tell them a mesmerizing story and transport them to another world.

Previous experience includes illustrations and concept work for private commissioners, Fandango, and 4th Dimension Entertainment.
